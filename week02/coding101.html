How to search for a good non-popular Anime 101

To find a good mass-unknown anime, there are certain procedures you must follow. Here I will list the proper procedures and explain the items needed to find it.

First and foremost, lets gather the items:

  • Computer or device with internet
  • Internet Connection
  • A seasoned Weeb
  • A list of genres you like
  • An account on a streaming site

Have you gathered all your items? (Yes/No)?

If yes, cool! If no, cool! Now the fun part begins. Here I will list the procedures to finding a good anime within the genre you like.

Follow this guide:

  1. Turn on internet device
  2. Go to a streaming service (i.e kissanime, crunchyroll, hulu, etc.)
  3. Navigate the home page (they usually have popular anime presented)
  4. Select 3 anime that seem interesting
  5. Check the genre
  6. Look for "similar anime"
  7. Search 3-4 times of similar anime list
If all else fails, search the genre you favored. Then search for those genres in the similar anime list.
